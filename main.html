Karat Place

Our Mission

We strive to provide women and their children, who are in need , a place to regain their strength. We also aid these women in the process of regaining their lives.

What are we?

We are a non-profit community outreach program geared to help women who have fallen on hard times. Our program also assists former convicted women gain employment.
